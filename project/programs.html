Programs Objective


The main Objective of the site is to bring the art community together in the form of the mural media. They has been a large growth in murals in Colorado in the last five years, this site focus in having a community mural base that artist and business can work together with the local community and school to have effective murals in the local neighborhoods. This mural mission is to have a positive impact in art and in the local youth community, so they can express them self in the paint media, and the use of walls as canvas. The large is Goal to have a large mural community to have a mural gallery with the help of local artist.
