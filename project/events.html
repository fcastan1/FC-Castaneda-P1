Café Cultura
...is an award-winning arts, culture and youth development organization that promotes unity and healing amoing indigenous peoples through creative expression while empowering youth to find their voice, reclaim oral and written traditions and become leaders in their communities. Café Cultura hosts an open mic night every second Friday at Denver Inner City Parish linking spoken word, visual art, dance and music with community involvement, education and healthy living. Their Xpress Yourself spoken word workshops continue to be among the most sought after for youth conferences and programs throughout Colorado. cafecultura.org"

Artstir Denver
